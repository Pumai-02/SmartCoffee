Our story

More than coffee.
It's community.

SmartCoffeeShop started in 2015 as a tiny cart outside a library. Our founder Maria Chen had one obsession: finding the world's best beans and turning them into the perfect cup.

We work directly with farms across six countries, paying above fair-trade prices and roasting every bag in-house.
